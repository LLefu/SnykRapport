Degree of Engagement Diagram explanation

The DoE diagram (Degree of engagement) diagram shows you how engaged organisations are in you're project. This ranges from the core group to the outer ring. The core group in this case are the organisations that are directly involved in the project. Every other ring's organisations are one step removed from the core process of the project.

Organizations involved in the project

Organizations involved with core project organizations

Organisations with no direct contact with the core project organisations

Organizations involved with non core organizations

The first degree speaks for, itself it shows the organisations directly involved in the project. For the other degree's more explanation is required. We take the other projects the core members are involved with and give back the other organisations involved in those projects. We do the same for the next degree's, while also excluding organisations from previous degree's. So each degree is one step removed from the organisations in the core group.

One's you have clicked on a project and a degree (one of the rings in the diagram) you will see a list of organisations. You can click on a organisation to get more information on them. You will be send to their own organisation page. You can also go too the project's page by clicking on 'go to project ->'.
